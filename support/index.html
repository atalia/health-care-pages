Last updated: July 23, 2026

Please include:

  • iPhone model and iOS version
  • Apple Watch model and watchOS version, if relevant
  • Whether HealthKit permissions are enabled for Aurora Vitals
  • Screenshots of the issue

HealthKit Data Does Not Appear

Check that the Apple Health app already contains the related samples, then confirm that Aurora Vitals has read access in the Health app or iOS Settings. Some cards need several days of Apple Watch and HealthKit samples before trends become useful.

Apple Watch, Widgets, Or Notifications Are Delayed

Apple Watch sync, widgets, watch faces, and local notifications are scheduled by the system and can be delayed. Check Low Power Mode, Focus, notification permissions, Background App Refresh, and whether the watch is paired and unlocked.

Pro Purchase Or Restore Issues

Aurora Vitals Pro is a one-time lifetime unlock processed by the App Store. If Pro content stays locked after purchase, open any locked card to reach the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the same Apple Account. Purchase problems such as billing or refunds are handled by Apple through Report a Problem.

Local Cache Reset

Data Management in Aurora Vitals clears local summaries, habit logs, recovery actions, and sync state stored by Aurora Vitals. It does not delete raw Apple Health samples.
